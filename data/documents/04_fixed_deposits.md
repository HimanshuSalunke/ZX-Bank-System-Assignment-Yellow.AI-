# ZX Bank — Fixed Deposits

## Overview

ZX Bank Fixed Deposits offer guaranteed returns with flexible tenure options from 7 days to 10 years. FDs are one of the safest investment instruments insured up to ₹5 Lakh by DICGC.

## Types of Fixed Deposits

### Regular FD
- Minimum deposit: ₹10,000
- Interest payout: Monthly, Quarterly, or at Maturity
- Auto-renewal available

### Tax Saver FD (Section 80C)
- Lock-in period: 5 years (no premature withdrawal)
- Maximum deduction: ₹1,50,000 per financial year
- Minimum deposit: ₹10,000
- Maximum deposit: ₹1,50,000

### Flexi FD
- Linked to current/savings account
- Sweep-in: Automatically breaks FD in multiples of ₹5,000 when account balance is low
- Sweep-out: Excess savings above a threshold automatically converted to FD
- Higher interest than savings, easy liquidity

### Recurring Deposit (RD)
- Monthly investment: ₹1,000 to ₹1,00,000
- Tenure: 6 months to 10 years
- Interest rate: Same as FD of equivalent tenure
- Premature closure allowed after 3 months

## Premature Withdrawal

- Allowed for Regular FD and Flexi FD
- Penalty: 1.00% reduction from applicable interest rate
- Not allowed for Tax Saver FD
- Partial withdrawal available in multiples of ₹5,000

## Loan Against FD

- Available: Up to 90% of FD value
- Interest rate: FD rate + 1.00%
- Processing fee: Nil
- Tenure: Up to the remaining FD tenure

## How to Open a Fixed Deposit

### Online
1. Log in to ZX Bank Internet/Mobile Banking
2. Navigate to Deposits → Open FD
3. Enter amount, tenure, and interest payout option
4. Confirm with OTP
5. FD created instantly

### At Branch
- Fill FD application form
- Provide cheque / debit from savings account
- FD receipt issued same day
