# ZX Bank — Business & Vehicle Loans

## Business Loans

### ZX MSME Loan
- **Amount**: ₹1 Lakh to ₹2 Crore
- **Interest Rate**: 11.00% – 14.00% p.a.
- **Tenure**: 12 to 60 months
- **Collateral**: Not required up to ₹50 Lakh (under CGTMSE)
- **Processing Fee**: 2% + GST
- **Eligibility**: Business vintage 2+ years, turnover above ₹10 Lakh

### Mudra Loan (PMMY)
- **Shishu**: Up to ₹50,000 (no collateral)
- **Kishore**: ₹50,001 to ₹5 Lakh (no collateral)
- **Tarun**: ₹5,00,001 to ₹10 Lakh
- **Interest Rate**: 8.50% – 12.00% p.a.
- **Purpose**: Working capital, equipment, business expansion

### Loan Against Property (LAP)
- **Amount**: ₹5 Lakh to ₹10 Crore
- **LTV**: Up to 65% of property value
- **Interest Rate**: 9.50% – 12.50% p.a.
- **Tenure**: Up to 15 years
- **Property Types**: Residential, commercial, industrial

## Vehicle Loans

### Car Loan
- **New Car**: Up to 100% on-road price
- **Used Car**: Up to 80% of valuation (max 5 years old)
- **Interest Rate**: 8.75% – 12.00% p.a.
- **Tenure**: 12 to 84 months
- **Processing Fee**: 0.50% (min ₹2,500)

### Two-Wheeler Loan
- **Amount**: Up to ₹3,00,000
- **Interest Rate**: 9.50% – 14.00% p.a.
- **Tenure**: 12 to 48 months
- **Processing Fee**: ₹1,000

### Commercial Vehicle Loan
- **New Vehicles**: Trucks, buses, tippers, trailers
- **Amount**: Up to ₹1 Crore
- **Interest Rate**: 10.00% – 14.00% p.a.
- **Tenure**: Up to 60 months
- **Down Payment**: Minimum 15%

## Gold Loan
- **Amount**: ₹25,000 to ₹50 Lakh
- **Interest Rate**: 7.50% – 10.00% p.a.
- **LTV**: Up to 75% of gold value
- **Tenure**: Up to 36 months
- **Disbursement**: Within 30 minutes
