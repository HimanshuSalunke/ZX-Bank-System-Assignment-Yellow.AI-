# ZX Bank — Terms, Policies & Regulatory Information

## Privacy Policy

ZX Bank is committed to protecting customer privacy:
- Personal data collected only for banking purposes
- Data shared with third parties only with consent or legal requirement
- Customers can request data deletion (subject to regulatory retention)
- Data stored in India as per RBI data localisation norms
- Full privacy policy: www.zxbank.co.in/privacy

## Fair Practices Code

In compliance with RBI guidelines:
- All loan terms disclosed clearly before disbursement
- No hidden charges — all fees listed in the Most Important Terms & Conditions (MITC)
- Customers have the right to prepay loans
- No coercive recovery practices
- Reasonable notice before any interest rate changes

## Interest Rate Policy

- **Savings Account**: Based on daily closing balance
- **Fixed Deposits**: Based on tenure (as published in rate card)
- **Loans**: Linked to RLLR (Repo Linked Lending Rate)
  - Current RLLR: **9.15% p.a.** (effective January 1, 2025)
  - RLLR reset: Every quarter (April 1, July 1, October 1, January 1)
  - Spread/margin fixed at sanction; does not change during loan tenure
- Interest calculation: Daily reducing balance method

## Deposit Insurance (DICGC)

- All deposits (savings, current, FD, RD) are insured
- Coverage: Up to **₹5,00,000** per depositor per bank
- Includes principal + interest
- Covers all branches of the same bank
- No action needed — insurance is automatic

As per RBI's Charter of Customer Rights:
1. **Right to Fair Treatment**: No discrimination
2. **Right to Transparency**: Clear disclosure of terms
3. **Right to Suitability**: Products recommended as per needs
4. **Right to Privacy**: Data protection
