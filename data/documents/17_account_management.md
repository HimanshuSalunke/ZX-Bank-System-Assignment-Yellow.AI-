# ZX Bank — Account Management Procedures

## KYC Update

### What is KYC?
Know Your Customer (KYC) is a mandatory process under RBI guidelines to verify customer identity and address. KYC must be updated periodically.

### Periodic KYC Re-Verification
- Every **2 years** for high-risk customers
- Every **8 years** for medium-risk customers
- Every **10 years** for low-risk customers
- Non-compliance may lead to account restrictions

### How to Update KYC
1. **Online (Video KYC)**: ZX Bank App → Settings → Update KYC → Video call
2. **Aadhaar-based e-KYC**: ZX Bank App → Settings → e-KYC → Aadhaar OTP
3. **Branch Visit**: Carry original Aadhaar + PAN to any ZX Bank branch

## Nomination

### Why Nominate?
Nomination ensures that in case of the account holder's demise, the nominee can claim the account balance/deposits/locker contents without legal complications.

### How to Add/Update Nominee
- **Online**: NetBanking → Service Requests → Nomination
- **Branch**: Fill Form DA-1 at any branch
- **Required details**: Nominee name, relationship, address, Aadhaar number
- **Multiple nominees**: Allowed for joint accounts (with percentage split)

---

## Joint Account Operations

### Types
- **Either or Survivor**: Any one can operate independently
- **Former or Survivor**: Only the first-named holder can operate
- **Jointly**: Both/all holders must sign for every transaction

### Tax Implications
- Interest is taxable for the first-named holder (or proportionally if declared)

---

## Account Closure

### Process
1. Withdraw all balance or transfer to another account
2. Surrender debit card, cheque book, passbook
3. Clear all pending dues (if any)
4. Fill account closure form at branch
5. Receive closure confirmation receipt

### Charges
- Within 14 days of opening: Free
- Within 1 year: ₹500
- After 1 year: Free

### Dormant/Inactive Account Reactivation
- Account inactive for **12 months**: Classified as dormant
- Dormant for **24 months**: Classified as inoperative
- **To reactivate**: Visit branch with KYC documents, submit reactivation request
- **Unclaimed deposits** (10+ years dormant): Transferred to RBI's DEAF fund
