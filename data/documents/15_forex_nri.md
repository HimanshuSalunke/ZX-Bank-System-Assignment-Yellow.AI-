# ZX Bank — Forex & NRI Services

## Foreign Exchange Services

### Forex Card (ZX Travel Card)
- Available currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, SGD, AED, AUD, CAD, JPY, THB, CHF
- Load amount: ₹25,000 to ₹25,00,000 equivalent
- Reload: Via branch or internet banking
- Validity: 5 years
- ATM withdrawal abroad: ₹125 per transaction
- Conversion markup: 2.5%
- Insurance: Complimentary travel insurance up to ₹10 Lakh

### Currency Exchange
- Buy/sell foreign currency notes at ZX Bank branches
- Available at 200+ branches across major cities
- Rates updated every 2 hours
- Carry limit: $3,000 equivalent in cash per trip

### Outward Remittance
- Wire transfer to 100+ countries
- Charges: ₹500 per transaction + correspondent bank fee
- Limit: Up to $250,000 per financial year (under LRS)
- Online via internet banking (for existing customers)

## NRI Banking

### NRE Savings Account (Non-Resident External)
- Currency: Indian Rupees (funded from foreign income)
- Interest: Tax-free in India
- Repatriation: Fully repatriable (principal + interest)
- Minimum balance: ₹10,000
- Joint account with another NRI allowed

### NRO Savings Account (Non-Resident Ordinary)
- Currency: Indian Rupees (Indian income — rent, dividends, pension)
- Interest: Taxable in India (TDS applicable)
- Repatriation: Up to $1 million per financial year (after tax clearance)
- Minimum balance: ₹10,000
- Joint account with resident Indian allowed

### FCNR(B) Deposit (Foreign Currency Non-Resident)
- Currency: USD, GBP, EUR, AUD, CAD, JPY
- Tenure: 1 year to 5 years
- Interest: Tax-free in India
- Repatriation: Fully repatriable
- Minimum deposit: $1,000 equivalent

### NRI Home Loan
- For property purchase in India
- Amount: Up to ₹5 Crore
- Interest rate: 8.75% – 9.50% p.a.
- Tenure: Up to 20 years
- LTV: Up to 80%

### How NRIs Can Open Accounts
1. **Online**: www.zxbank.co.in/nri → Apply
2. **At Indian branches**: During India visit with passport + visa
3. **Embassy**: Through Indian embassy (select countries)
4. **Documents**: Passport, employment proof, overseas address proof, PAN card
