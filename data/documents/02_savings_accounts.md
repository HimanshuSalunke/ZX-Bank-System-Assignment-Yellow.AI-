# ZX Bank — Savings Accounts

## ZX Regular Savings Account

A basic savings account designed for salaried individuals and students.

### Features
- Zero minimum balance (for salary accounts)
- Free debit card (RuPay / Visa Classic)
- Internet and mobile banking included
- Free NEFT/RTGS/IMPS transfers
- SMS alerts for all transactions

### Eligibility
- Indian resident, aged 18 years or above
- Valid KYC documents (Aadhaar + PAN mandatory)

### Interest Rate
- Up to ₹1 Lakh: **3.00% p.a.**
- ₹1 Lakh to ₹10 Lakh: **3.50% p.a.**
- Above ₹10 Lakh: **4.00% p.a.**

### Charges
- Minimum balance (non-salary): ₹5,000 (Metro), ₹2,500 (Semi-Urban), ₹1,000 (Rural)
- Non-maintenance charges: ₹500 per quarter
- Cheque book: First 25 leaves free per year, ₹3/leaf thereafter

## ZX Premium Savings Account

A premium account with enhanced benefits for high-net-worth individuals.

### Features
- Complimentary Visa Platinum debit card with ₹5 Lakh daily ATM limit
- Priority banking with dedicated relationship manager
- Free locker allocation (subject to availability)
- Unlimited free NEFT/RTGS transfers
- Complimentary airport lounge access (4 per year)
- Free demand drafts up to ₹50,000 per month
- Enhanced daily POS limit: ₹5 Lakh

### Eligibility
- Maintain Average Monthly Balance (AMB) of ₹2,00,000 or above
- Or maintain a Fixed Deposit of ₹10,00,000 or above

### Interest Rate
- All balances: **5.00% p.a.**

### Charges
- Non-maintenance of AMB: ₹1,000 per quarter
- No charges on any digital transactions

## How to Open a Savings Account

### Online (Recommended)
1. Visit www.zxbank.co.in/open-account
2. Enter Aadhaar and PAN details
3. Complete Video KYC (5 minutes)
4. Account activated within 30 minutes
5. Debit card delivered within 5 business days

### At Branch
1. Visit any ZX Bank branch
2. Fill the Account Opening Form
3. Submit KYC documents (originals for verification)
4. Initial deposit (if applicable)
5. Account activated within 24 hours

### Documents Required
- Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- Passport-size photograph (2 copies)
- Address proof (if different from Aadhaar)
