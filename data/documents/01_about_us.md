# ZX Bank — About Us

## Overview

ZX Bank is one of India's leading private sector banks, established in 1994 and headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. With over 2,500 branches across 28 states and 8 union territories, ZX Bank serves more than 15 million retail and corporate customers.

## Mission

To be the most trusted financial partner, delivering innovative banking solutions that empower individuals and businesses to achieve their financial goals.

## Vision

A digitally-driven bank that combines world-class technology with personalised service to create exceptional customer experiences.

## Core Values

- **Integrity**: Transparent and ethical practices in every transaction
- **Innovation**: Leveraging technology to simplify banking
- **Inclusion**: Making banking accessible to all sections of society
- **Customer First**: Every decision starts with the customer
