# ZX Bank — Security & Fraud Prevention

## Security Measures

### Account Security
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** for all online transactions
- **Biometric login** (fingerprint, face recognition) on mobile app
- **Device binding**: Only registered devices can access your account
- **Session management**: Auto-logout after 5 minutes of inactivity
- **Virtual keyboard**: Prevents keylogger attacks on internet banking
- **End-to-end encryption**: 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption

### Transaction Security
- **OTP verification** for all financial transactions above ₹2,000
- **Cooling period**: 24 hours for new beneficiary first transaction
- **Transaction alerts**: Instant SMS + email for every debit/credit
- **Daily limits**: Configurable per channel (mobile/net banking/ATM)
- **3D Secure**: Mandatory for all online card transactions

### Card Security
- **EMV chip cards**: All cards are chip-enabled for secure POS transactions
- **Contactless limit**: ₹5,000 per transaction without PIN
- **Magnetic stripe**: Disabled by default for international cards
- **CVV masking**: CVV not printed on card (view in app only)
- **Token-based payments**: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay

## Common Fraud Types & Prevention

### Phishing
- **What it is**: Fake emails/SMS pretending to be from ZX Bank
- **How to identify**: Check sender domain, look for spelling errors, urgent tone
- **Prevention**: ZX Bank will NEVER ask for PIN, OTP, CVV, or password via email/SMS/call

### Vishing (Phone Fraud)
- **What it is**: Fraudsters calling as "bank officials" asking for card details
- **Prevention**: Never share OTP, PIN, or card number over phone. ZX Bank staff will never ask for these.

### SIM Swap Fraud
- **What it is**: Fraudster duplicates your SIM to receive OTPs
- **Prevention**: If your SIM stops working suddenly, immediately contact your telecom provider AND call ZX Bank

### UPI Fraud
- **What it is**: Fraudster sends a "collect money" request disguised as a payment
- **Prevention**: Never approve unknown collect requests. You NEVER need to enter PIN to receive money.

### Card Skimming
- **What it is**: Device attached to ATM card slot copies your card data
- **Prevention**: Check for loose attachments on ATM machines, cover PIN pad while entering PIN

## Reporting Fraud

### Immediate Steps
1. **Block your card/account**: Call 1800-200-9925 (24/7) or use mobile app
2. **File complaint**: Visit nearest branch or email fraud@zxbank.co.in
3. **FIR**: File an FIR at the nearest police station
4. **RBI**: Report on RBI's Sachet portal (https://sachet.rbi.org.in)

### Zero Liability Policy
- Transactions reported within **3 days**: Full refund within 10 working days
- Reported within **4–7 days**: Liability limited to ₹10,000
- Reported after **7 days**: As per bank's Board-approved policy

### Dispute Resolution Timeline
- **Acknowledgement**: Within 24 hours
- **Resolution**: Within 90 days (as per RBI guidelines)
- **Credit of refund**: Within 10 working days of resolution
