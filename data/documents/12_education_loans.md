# ZX Bank — Education Loans

## Overview

ZX Bank Education Loans support higher education in India and abroad, covering tuition fees, living expenses, travel, and equipment costs.

## Loan Parameters

| Parameter | India | Abroad |
|---|---|---|
| Loan Amount | Up to ₹20 Lakh | Up to ₹1 Crore |
| Collateral Required | Above ₹7.50 Lakh | Above ₹7.50 Lakh |
| Interest Rate | 9.50% – 11.00% p.a. | 10.00% – 11.50% p.a. |
| Moratorium | Course duration + 1 year | Course duration + 6 months |
| Repayment Tenure | Up to 15 years after moratorium |
| Processing Fee | ₹10,000 (fixed) | ₹15,000 (fixed) |

## Courses Covered

- Engineering (B.Tech, M.Tech)
- Medical (MBBS, MD, MS)
- MBA / PGDM (Top 100 B-schools)
- Sciences (M.Sc., Ph.D.)
- Law (LLB, LLM)
- Chartered Accountancy (CA)
- Design, Architecture, Fine Arts

## Expenses Covered

- Tuition fees and examination fees
- Hostel and mess charges
- Books, equipment, and laptop (up to ₹50,000)
- Travel expenses (for abroad — one-way economy)
- Caution deposit / building fund
- Insurance premium (if mandatory)

## Eligibility

- Indian citizen
- Secured admission in recognised institution
- Age: No upper limit (student must have a co-applicant)
- Academic: Minimum 50% in qualifying examination
- Co-applicant: Parent / guardian with stable income source

## Documents Required

- Admission letter from institution
- Fee structure
- Mark sheets (10th, 12th, graduation)
- KYC of student and co-applicant
- Income proof of co-applicant
- Collateral documents (if applicable)

## Tax Benefits

- Interest on education loan is fully deductible under **Section 80E**
- No upper limit on deduction
- Available for 8 years from the year of first repayment
- Applicable for loans from recognised financial institutions only
