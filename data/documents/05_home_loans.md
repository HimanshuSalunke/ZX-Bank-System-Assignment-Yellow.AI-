# ZX Bank — Home Loans

## Overview

ZX Bank Home Loans help you purchase your dream home with competitive interest rates, flexible tenures, and quick disbursement. We finance residential properties, including apartments, villas, plots, and construction.

## Interest Rates

| Category | Interest Rate (p.a.) |
|---|---|
| Salaried — Up to ₹30 Lakh | 8.50% |
| Salaried — ₹30 Lakh to ₹75 Lakh | 8.75% |
| Salaried — Above ₹75 Lakh | 9.00% |
| Self-Employed — Up to ₹30 Lakh | 8.75% |
| Self-Employed — ₹30 Lakh to ₹75 Lakh | 9.00% |
| Self-Employed — Above ₹75 Lakh | 9.25% |
| Women borrowers | 0.05% concession on above rates |

> Interest rates are linked to ZX Bank RLLR (Repo Linked Lending Rate). Current RLLR: 9.15%

## Loan Details

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| Loan Amount | ₹5 Lakh to ₹5 Crore |
| Tenure | 5 years to 30 years |
| LTV Ratio | Up to 80% of property value (90% for loans up to ₹30 Lakh) |
| Processing Fee | 0.50% of loan amount (max ₹25,000) |
| Prepayment Charges | Nil for floating rate loans |
| Disbursement | Within 7 working days of document verification |

## Eligibility

### Salaried Applicants
- Age: 23 to 60 years (at loan maturity)
- Minimum income: ₹25,000 per month
- Minimum work experience: 2 years (1 year with current employer)
- CIBIL score: 700 or above

### Self-Employed Applicants
- Age: 25 to 65 years (at loan maturity)
- Minimum income: ₹3,00,000 per annum (as per ITR)
- Business vintage: Minimum 3 years
- CIBIL score: 700 or above

## Documents Required

### Salaried
- Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
- Address proof
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Latest 3 months salary slips
- Form 16 / ITR for last 2 years
- Property documents (agreement, title deed, approved plan)
- Processing fee cheque

### Self-Employed
- Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
- Business registration documents
- Last 3 years ITR with computation of income
- Last 6 months bank statements (personal + business)
- Balance sheet and P&L for last 3 years (audited)
- Property documents
