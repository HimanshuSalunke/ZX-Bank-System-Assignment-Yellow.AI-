# ZX Bank — Personal Loans

## Overview

ZX Bank Personal Loans provide instant funds for any purpose — medical emergencies, weddings, travel, home renovation, education, or debt consolidation. No collateral required.

## Interest Rates

| Category | Interest Rate (p.a.) |
|---|---|
| Salaried (CIBIL 750+) | 10.49% |
| Salaried (CIBIL 700-749) | 11.99% |
| Self-Employed (CIBIL 750+) | 12.49% |
| Self-Employed (CIBIL 700-749) | 13.99% |
| Existing ZX Bank Customers | 0.50% concession |

## Loan Parameters

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| Loan Amount | ₹50,000 to ₹40,00,000 |
| Tenure | 12 months to 60 months |
| Processing Fee | 2.00% of loan amount + GST |
| Foreclosure Charges | 4% of outstanding (after 12 EMIs; NIL after 24 EMIs) |
| Disbursement | Within 4 hours for pre-approved; 48 hours otherwise |

## Eligibility

- **Age**: 21 to 58 years (salaried), 25 to 65 years (self-employed)
- **Income**: Minimum ₹20,000/month (salaried), ₹3,00,000/year (self-employed)
- **CIBIL Score**: Minimum 700
- **Employment**: Minimum 1 year total, 6 months with current employer

## Documents Required

- PAN Card and Aadhaar Card
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Address proof

## Pre-Approved Personal Loans

Existing ZX Bank savings/salary account holders may receive pre-approved offers:
- Instant disbursement in **under 2 minutes**
- Zero documentation
- Available on ZX Bank Mobile App → Loans → Pre-Approved Offers
- Interest rates starting from **9.99% p.a.**
