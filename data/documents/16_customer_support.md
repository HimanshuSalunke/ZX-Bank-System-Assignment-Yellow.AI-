## Grievance Redressal

### Level 1: Branch / Customer Care
- Register complaint at branch or call 1800-200-9925
- Complaint reference number provided
- Resolution timeline: **7 working days**

### Level 2: Nodal Officer
- If not resolved in Level 1, escalate to Nodal Officer
- Email: nodal.officer@zxbank.co.in
- Write to: Nodal Officer, ZX Bank, BKC, Mumbai — 400051
- Resolution timeline: **15 working days**

### Level 3: Banking Ombudsman (RBI)
- If unresolved after 30 days or unsatisfied with resolution
- File complaint on RBI CMS: https://cms.rbi.org.in
- Or write to Banking Ombudsman of your region
- No cost to the complainant
