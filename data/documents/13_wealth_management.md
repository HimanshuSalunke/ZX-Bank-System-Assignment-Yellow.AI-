# ZX Bank — Wealth Management & Investments

## Mutual Funds

ZX Bank offers mutual fund investments from 30+ AMCs through its platform.

### How to Invest
- **Mobile App**: ZX Bank App → Invest → Mutual Funds
- **Internet Banking**: NetBanking → Investments → Mutual Funds
- **Branch**: Visit any ZX Bank branch

### SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)
- Minimum SIP: ₹500/month
- Frequencies: Monthly, Quarterly, Weekly
- Step-up SIP: Increase SIP by fixed % annually
- Pause SIP: Up to 3 months
- Auto-debit from ZX Bank savings account

### Fund Categories Available
- Equity (Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap, Flexi Cap)
- Debt (Liquid, Short Duration, Corporate Bond, Gilt)
- Hybrid (Balanced Advantage, Aggressive Hybrid)
- Index Funds & ETFs
- ELSS (Tax Saving — Section 80C)
- International Funds
