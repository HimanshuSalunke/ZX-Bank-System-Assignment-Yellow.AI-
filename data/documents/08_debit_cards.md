# ZX Bank — Debit Cards

## Types of Debit Cards

### ZX RuPay Classic
- Annual fee: Free
- Daily ATM withdrawal limit: ₹25,000
- Daily POS limit: ₹1,00,000
- Online transaction limit: ₹50,000
- Domestic use only

### ZX Visa Classic
- Annual fee: ₹200
- Daily ATM withdrawal limit: ₹50,000
- Daily POS limit: ₹2,00,000
- Online transaction limit: ₹1,00,000
- International usage enabled

### ZX Visa Platinum
- Annual fee: ₹500 (free with Premium Savings Account)
- Daily ATM withdrawal limit: ₹1,00,000
- Daily POS limit: ₹5,00,000
- Online transaction limit: ₹3,00,000
- International usage enabled
- Airport lounge access: 2 per quarter
- Purchase protection insurance

## ATM Charges

- **ZX Bank ATM**: Unlimited free transactions
- **Other bank ATM (Metro)**: 3 free per month, ₹21 per transaction thereafter
- **Other bank ATM (Non-Metro)**: 5 free per month, ₹21 per transaction thereafter

## Card Controls

Through ZX Bank Mobile App, you can:
- Enable/disable international transactions
- Enable/disable online transactions
- Set custom daily limits
- Block card instantly
- Enable/disable contactless payments
- Set merchant category restrictions

## Lost or Stolen Card

1. **Immediate**: Call 1800-200-9925 (24/7) to block the card
2. **Mobile App**: ZX Bank App → Cards → Block Card
3. **SMS**: Send BLOCK XXXX (last 4 digits) to 56161
4. Replacement card delivered within 5 business days
5. Zero liability protection for unauthorised transactions reported within 3 days
