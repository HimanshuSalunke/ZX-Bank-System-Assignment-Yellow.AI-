# ZX Bank — Credit Cards

## ZX Classic Credit Card

An entry-level card for everyday spending.

### Features
- Annual fee: ₹500 (waived on spending ₹50,000 p.a.)
- Reward points: 1 point per ₹100 spent
- Fuel surcharge waiver (1% up to ₹500/month)
- Credit limit: ₹25,000 to ₹2,00,000
- EMI conversion available on purchases above ₹2,500
- Contactless payments enabled

### Interest Rate
- Monthly: 3.49% (41.88% p.a.)
- Interest-free period: Up to 50 days

---

## ZX Gold Credit Card

Enhanced rewards and travel benefits for frequent spenders.

### Features
- Annual fee: ₹1,500 (waived on spending ₹1,50,000 p.a.)
- Reward points: 2 points per ₹100 spent (4x on dining and travel)
- Complimentary domestic airport lounge access: 4 per quarter
- Travel insurance: ₹25 Lakh
- Credit limit: ₹1,00,000 to ₹5,00,000
- 1% cashback on utility bill payments

---

## ZX Platinum Credit Card

Premium card with lifestyle benefits.

### Features
- Annual fee: ₹5,000 (waived on spending ₹5,00,000 p.a.)
- Reward points: 5 points per ₹100 spent (10x on partner merchants)
- Complimentary international airport lounge access: 8 per year
- Golf privileges: 2 complimentary rounds per quarter
- Concierge service: 24/7
- Credit limit: ₹3,00,000 to ₹15,00,000
- Comprehensive travel insurance: ₹1 Crore
- Purchase protection: 90 days

---

## ZX Business Credit Card

Designed for business expenses and corporate spending.

### Features
- Annual fee: ₹2,500
- Reward points: 3 points per ₹100 on business categories
- Free supplementary cards for employees (up to 5)
- Detailed expense reports and analytics
- GST input credit tracking
- Credit limit: ₹2,00,000 to ₹25,00,000

## Credit Card Charges

| Service | Charges |
|---|---|
| Cash advance fee | 2.5% of amount (min ₹500) |
| Late payment fee | ₹100 to ₹1,300 (based on outstanding) |
| Over-limit fee | 2.5% of over-limit amount |
| Foreign currency markup | 3.5% of transaction value |
| Card replacement | ₹200 (lost/stolen) |
| EMI conversion fee | ₹199 per conversion |
| Reward point value | 1 point = ₹0.25 |
