# ZX Bank — Government Schemes & Priority Sector

## Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)
- Zero balance savings account
- Free RuPay debit card with ₹2 Lakh accident insurance
- Overdraft facility up to ₹10,000 (after 6 months satisfactory operation)
- Life insurance cover: ₹30,000
- Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) enabled

## Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)
- See Business Loans section for detailed rates and categories
- Shishu (up to ₹50,000), Kishore (up to ₹5 Lakh), Tarun (up to ₹10 Lakh)

## Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)
- For girl child (below 10 years)
- Interest rate: 8.20% p.a. (reviewed quarterly)
- Minimum deposit: ₹250/year
- Maximum deposit: ₹1,50,000/year
- Maturity: 21 years from opening
- Tax benefit: Section 80C + Interest tax-free + Maturity tax-free (EEE)

## Atal Pension Yojana (APY)
- Monthly pension of ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 after age 60
- Age: 18 to 40 years
- Government co-contribution for eligible subscribers
- Auto-debit from ZX Bank savings account

## Senior Citizen Savings Scheme (SCSS)
- Age: 60 years and above (55+ for retired defence personnel)
- Interest rate: 8.20% p.a. (quarterly payout)
- Maximum deposit: ₹30 Lakh
- Tenure: 5 years (extendable by 3 years)
- Tax benefit: Section 80C (up to ₹1.50 Lakh)

## Public Provident Fund (PPF)
- Interest rate: 7.10% p.a. (compounded annually)
- Minimum deposit: ₹500/year
- Maximum deposit: ₹1,50,000/year
- Lock-in: 15 years (partial withdrawal after 7 years)
- Tax benefit: EEE (Section 80C)

## PM Vishwakarma Scheme
- For traditional artisans and craftspeople
- Collateral-free loan up to ₹3 Lakh (in 2 tranches)
- Interest rate: 5% p.a. (Government subsidised)
- Skills training and toolkit support
