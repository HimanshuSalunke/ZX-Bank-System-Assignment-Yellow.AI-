# ZX Bank — Current Accounts

## ZX Business Current Account

Designed for small and medium enterprises, sole proprietors, and traders.

### Features
- Unlimited free transactions (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS)
- Free cash deposits up to ₹5 Lakh per month
- Complimentary business debit card (Visa Business)
- Access to business internet banking with bulk payment facility
- Integration with popular accounting software (Tally, QuickBooks)
- Overdraft facility available

### Eligibility
- Registered business entity (Proprietorship, Partnership, Company, LLP)
- Valid GST registration (for businesses with turnover above ₹20 Lakh)
- KYC of all partners/directors

### Minimum Balance
- Average Quarterly Balance (AQB): ₹25,000
- Non-maintenance: ₹1,000 per quarter

## ZX Corporate Current Account

For large enterprises and institutions with high-volume banking needs.

### Features
- Dedicated corporate banking team
- Cash management services (collections, payments, pooling)
- Trade finance integration (LC, BG, export/import)
- API banking for ERP integration
- Multi-level transaction authorisation
- Virtual accounts for automated reconciliation
- Free cash handling up to ₹20 Lakh per month

### Eligibility
- Companies with annual turnover above ₹10 Crore
- Board resolution for account opening
- KYC of all authorised signatories

### Minimum Balance
- AQB: ₹5,00,000
- Non-maintenance: ₹5,000 per quarter

---

## ZX Start-Up Current Account

Special zero-balance account for DPIIT-recognised start-ups.

### Features
- Zero minimum balance for first 2 years
- Free 500 NEFT/RTGS transactions per month
- Collateral-free overdraft up to ₹25 Lakh (subject to approval)
- Integration with payment gateways (Razorpay, PayU, Cashfree)
- Free GST invoicing tool
- Access to ZX Bank Start-Up Accelerator programme

### Eligibility
- DPIIT recognition certificate
- Business less than 5 years old
- Entity registered under Companies Act or LLP Act
